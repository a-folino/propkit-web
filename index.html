Quick FAQ

Is this financial advice?
No. PropKit provides estimates only and is not financial or tax advice. Always confirm with official state calculators and a licensed professional.

Why might stamp duty differ from a state site?
Thresholds and concessions change regularly. Ensure your contract date, buyer type, property type and value match the official inputs.

Where does my data go?
Calculations run on-device. Your inputs stay on your device unless you choose to share.
