PropKit – Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 30th, 2025

PropKit is a calculator app for property stats and information. We do not collect, store, or share personal data.

Data Collection

  • We do not collect personal information.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • Any inputs you enter are processed on-device and may be stored locally on your device (e.g., preferences). This data never leaves your device.

Permissions

PropKit does not request device permissions beyond what is required for normal app operation.

Children’s Privacy

PropKit is not directed to children under 13 and collects no personal information.

Changes

If we change this policy, we’ll update this page and the “Last updated” date.
